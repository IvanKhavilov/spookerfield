What is this place?

So, another poor lost soul has come to us in the border world

You know, we have quite a lot of secrets, the only thing you are allowed to know now is written below.

Our village cannot be found on the maps, only the chosen ones get here, however, do not be happy that you got here, this does not mean that you will be able to leave here

You are probably wondering what kind of creature is that on the left?

Someone named Spooker

As I managed to find out - this zone is controlled by some creature, we do not know where it came from, but we know for sure that it decides who will leave Spookerfield with honors and glory, and who will become a victim of these places.

We call this creature Spooker. So far, we have managed to find out that Spooker loves gambling, or rather poker, so he often organizes tournaments here. Unfortunately, poker is not played for chips here...
